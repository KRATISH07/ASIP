EMERGENCY RESPONSE PROTOCOLS — ASIP SOCIETY
============================================

Protocol 1: CRITICAL WATER SHORTAGE
--------------------------------------
Trigger: Tank level below 5% OR pressure below 0.5 bar

Immediate Actions (0-15 minutes):
1. Alert society manager via automated SMS and email
2. Broadcast PA announcement: "Dear residents, we are experiencing a water shortage.
   Our team is working to resolve this. Estimated restoration: [TIME]"
3. Contact water tanker supplier (emergency contract number on file)
4. Dispatch maintenance staff to pump room

Short-term Actions (15-60 minutes):
1. Arrange emergency water supply via tankers
2. Notify Resident Welfare Association (RWA) committee
3. Post physical notices at lift lobbies and notice boards
4. Identify root cause (pump failure vs supply issue)

Resolution Actions:
1. Restore supply, test pressure at all floors
2. Send resolution notification to all residents
3. File incident report within 24 hours
4. Update maintenance log

Protocol 2: POWER OUTAGE
--------------------------
Trigger: Power consumption drops to 0 kW

Immediate Actions (0-5 minutes):
1. Automatic DG set activation
2. Verify DG fuel level (minimum 50% required)
3. Alert electrician on duty
4. Check if outage is tower-specific or society-wide

Communication (within 10 minutes):
- SMS to all residents: "Power disruption detected. DG backup active. Team working on restoration."
- Email to RWA with technical details

Protocol 3: TANK OVERFLOW
---------------------------
Trigger: Tank level above 95%

Immediate Actions:
1. Close inlet valve immediately
2. Alert pump operator
3. Check overflow drain to prevent water damage
4. Inspect float valve for failure

Protocol 4: MULTI-INCIDENT ESCALATION
---------------------------------------
If two or more critical incidents occur simultaneously:
1. Escalate to Society Committee President
2. Contact backup contractors immediately
3. Prioritize life-safety systems (lifts, emergency lighting)
4. Consider engaging municipal corporation for support
